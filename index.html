Welcome to

A web application developed to leverage machine learning for fluvial flood prediction for the municipality of Camaligan. Our goal is to empower communities with accurate and timely information to mitigate the risks associated with flooding.

Explore the Flood Extent Map to see our model's flood predictions and stay informed with our warning system.
