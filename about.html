FLOWS is a predictive fluvial flood mapping web application developed for the Municipality of Camaligan, Camarines Sur

Rooted in machine learning and GIS, this platform enables timely flood predictions—up to six hours in advance—empowering communities to prepare and respond effectively. This web application was conceptualized as part of an undergraduate thesis at Bicol University College of Science.
